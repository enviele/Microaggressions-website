About this Website

This is a page to inform people about microaggressions. The focus is mainly on microaggressions on college campuses to emphasize that these events are not exclusive to non-college settings. Although colleges and unversities are portrayed as progressive and welcoming environments, these situations are still present on campuses because these are settings that include people of varying exposure to diversity. As much as high levels of diversity are advertised to the public, it's the lack of true diversity that is the main reason for the rise of these incidents that marginalized group of students experience. This website is meant to give insight into what different college students have experienced even within this advertised safe environment.
