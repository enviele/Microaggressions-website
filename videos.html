This is a very simplified but accurate representation of what microaggressions are like for people who experience them on the daily. It may seem like an overreaction to an outsider who is just tuning in to the last straw when in reality it’s the accumulation of the everyday slights and comments that result in that reaction. Minorities are taught to simply ignore or move on because it’s not that big of a deal. This video explores why microaggressions are such an important topic to discuss.

The history of how the awareness of microaggressions came to be is explained in this video. From the coining of the term to the study of how microaggressions affect minorities, this is a summary of how this came such an important topic that is a current point of discussion.

This is an interview with Dr. Sue who provided the definition of microaggressions that I gave to all the people I interviewed.
